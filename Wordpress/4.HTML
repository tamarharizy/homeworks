who is charlotte tilbury

Charlotte Tilbury MBE is a British makeup artist.
She is the founder, chairperson, president, and chief creative officer of the makeup and skincare brand Charlotte Tilbury Beauty Ltd.
Tilbury is a contributing beauty editor for British Vogue and global ambassador for Women International.
